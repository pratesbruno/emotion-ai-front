Facial Emotion Recognition

Final project @Le Wagon

How are you feeling right now?

Realtime predictions on facial expressions

on the top left corner you can see a real time prediction of your predominant emotion. the model uses your facial expressions to predict one of the four emotions - neutral, happy, sad or suprised. Therefore we built our own convolutional neural network with 4 convolutional and maxpooling layers and added dropout layers for regularization purposes. Further we trained the model on the fer2013 and mma dataset from kaggle. To enhance accuracy we used data augmentation techniques to artificially create more training data. further we created an ensemble of five very similar convolutional neural networks. each of the five neural network creates its one prediction and the final prediction is generated by the mean of all individual predictions.

Predicting emotions

We used opencv and tensorflow js to realize this project

Based on your webcam stream, we extract faces with a cascade sheet. This picture is transformed to fit into our convolutional neural network, which we pretrained in python. The saved pretrained model, was transformed to a json file, which allowed us to use it in tensorflow js. Tensorflow js gets executed directly your browser.
