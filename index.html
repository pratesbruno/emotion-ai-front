Predicting emotions

Using opencv and tensorflow js

Now based on your webcam stream, we extract faces with a cascade sheet from the opencv library. This picture gets transformed into a model in tensorflow js. We built our own Convolutional neural network with 4 convolutional and maxpooling layers and added dropout layers. Further we trained the model on the fer2013 and mma dataset from kaggle. To enhance accuracy we used data augmentation technique to virtually create more trainings data. Just hit the load button and you will see your emotion recognition in realtime.
